DANNI:
  ◉ Feels like a living presence
  ◉ Breathing is barely perceptible but subconsciously calming
  ◉ Warmth spreads naturally, like candlelight
  ◉ Timing feels "human" - never quite predictable
  ◉ Evokes: Trust, warmth, intelligence with empathy

SubFrac.OS:
  ◉ Feels like a system process
  ◉ Pulse is mechanical, predictable, tireless
  ◉ Effects snap into place with digital precision
  ◉ Timing is metronomic - you could set a clock by it
  ◉ Evokes: Power, precision, intelligence without emotion

Together:
  ◉ Two different kinds of intelligence
  ◉ Complementary, not competing
  ◉ Visual "conversation" suggests collaboration
  ◉ Contrast makes both more distinctive
  ◉ User understands the duality without reading a word
