# Hero Section Creative Brief

## Concept: "Anatomy of Place"

Brisbane city map + Vitruvian Man overlay — the city rendered as a living system, human at the center.

---

## The Idea

Subfracture is a Culture Studio based in Brisbane. The hero visualizes their philosophy: **understanding the anatomy of culture, seeing the systems beneath the surface.**

The Vitruvian Man represents Renaissance ideals — the human as measure of all things, art and science unified. Overlaid on Brisbane's street grid, it says: *we diagnose cities, brands, and culture with the same precision Da Vinci brought to the human form.*

## Visual Execution

### Layers (back to front)

1. **Cream/off-white background** — warm, paper-like
2. **Brisbane map** — terracotta linework, subtle, acts as texture/system
3. **Sacred geometry** — circle and square from Vitruvian original, thin lines
4. **Vitruvian figure** — centered, arms extended, line drawing style
5. **Typography** — "Culture Studio" headline, editorial serif

### Composition

- Figure centered in viewport
- Arms extend toward edges (but don't touch — breathing room)
- Brisbane River could align with figure's central vertical axis
- Sacred geometry circles frame the figure
- Map visible through/around the figure (not behind as solid block)

### Style Notes

- **Linework**: Thin, precise, hand-drawn feel (not vector-perfect)
- **Aging**: Subtle paper texture, not pristine digital
- **Color**: Terracotta `~#c55a3d` primary, black for figure, cream `~#f5f0e8` background
- **Feel**: Renaissance sketchbook meets urban planning document

---

## Animation Concept (Optional)

On page load or scroll:

1. Map fades in first (the system)
2. Sacred geometry draws itself (circles, then square)
3. Vitruvian figure draws from center outward (the human emerges)
4. Typography fades in last

**Interaction**: Subtle parallax — map and figure on different layers, slight depth on mouse move or scroll.
