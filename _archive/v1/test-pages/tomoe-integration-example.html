The Tomoe Symbol

The Tomoe is an ancient Japanese symbol representing the flow of energy, cycles of nature, and the interconnection of forces. Its three-way spiral embodies the continuous movement and balance that defines natural systems.

For Subfracture, the Tomoe represents our approach: where art and systems flow together, where strategy and creativity spiral in harmony, where culture emerges from the intersection of multiple forces.

This self-drawing animation captures that moment of emergence - watching the symbol come into being, line by line, until the pattern reveals itself.
