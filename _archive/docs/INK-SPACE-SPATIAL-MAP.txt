═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════
                              PARTICLE SYSTEM
═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

  Count: 25,000 (desktop) / 8,000 (mobile)

  Movement: Curl noise - fluid, organic drift

  Behavior: Particles flow around drawings, creating atmosphere

  Appearance: Soft white glow, additive blending, 40% opacity

  Wrapping: Particles reset when drifting beyond bounds (-30 to 30 x/y)

  Purpose: Mystery, depth perception, ethereal quality


═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════
                              FOG SYSTEM
═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

  Type: Exponential fog (FogExp2)

  Density: 0.015 (increases quadratically with distance)

  Color: #0a0a0a (matches background)

  Effect:
    - Near (z = 0 to -20): Clear, drawings fully visible
    - Mid (z = -20 to -60): Atmospheric, slight obscurity
    - Far (z = -60 to -100): Heavy fog, barely visible

  Purpose: Progressive mystery, can't see everything at once


═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════
                            CAMERA BEHAVIOR
═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

  Start: z = 0, y = 0, x = 0

  End: z = -100, y = varies, x = varies

  Movement: Bound to scroll progress via GSAP ScrollTrigger

  Easing: power2.out (smooth, natural)

  Organic drift:
    - x: sin(progress * 2π) * 2  (side-to-side sway)
    - y: cos(progress * 3π) * 1.5 (vertical drift)

  Purpose: Not clinical camera movement - feels like floating


═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════
                              DESIGN NOTES
═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

  1. Drawings are not centered - intentional asymmetry creates discovery

  2. Rotation angles vary - no two planes face exactly forward

  3. Subtle floating - each plane bobs at different rates (0.3-0.5 speed)

  4. Scale matters - larger pieces demand more attention

  5. Fog obscures far depths - mystery is intentional

  6. Particles never settle - constant organic movement

  7. Camera sway - prevents mechanical feeling

  8. Opacity at 90% - drawings feel slightly ethereal, not solid

  9. No harsh lighting - soft ambient + directional creates mood

 10. Black background - void, not space; consciousness, not gallery
